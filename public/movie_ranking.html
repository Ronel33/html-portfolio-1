The Best Movies According to Sassy

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Pirates of the Caribbean

I love all the movies, because Johny Dep played Jack Sparrow so well and the humour in the movies is really fun.

The Pope's Exorcist

The story is about Gabriele Amorth, the Pope's designated exorcist and based on a true story. It keeps you on the edge of your chair.

Mortal Engines

This was a post-apocalyptic steampunk action packed movie which I really enjoyed.
